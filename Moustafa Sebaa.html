here you'll find a piece of information about myself; who am I, what I am doing, what's inspiring & driving me forward, and what I would love to do next !!


I am a PhD Candidate in the 3SR Laboratory at the University of Grenoble Alpes. I hold a Master degree in Engineering from USTHB in Algeria, after which I moved to France to do my second MSc degree of research at UPPA. The corresponding research internships had completely different applications but were complementary in term of making a better understanding of Physics in porous materials and complex systems. My current research deals with granular materials. Discrete Element Method will be applied to study the deformation of granular systems. Check the Research section for more information.
